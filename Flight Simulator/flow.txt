FlowChart

1. Plane selection
    a. Plane name (Origin)

    b. Plane info
        I. VR Rotation Speed
        II. VREF Landing Speed
        III. VLO Landing Gear Speed
        IV. VS Stall Speed
        V. Max Passengers
        VI. Max Cargo
        VII. Max flight time estimated

2. Destination selection
    a. Estimated Flight time from Point A to Point B
    b. Distance
    c. Plane selected
    d. Airport Code
    e. Airport Name

3.Ground Systems
    a. Systems
        I. Ground Power Unit
        II. ACU
        III. Pushback

    b. Load
        I. Pax
        II. Cargo
        III. Meals
        IV. Fuel

4. Aircraft Systems
    a. Systems
        I. Main battery
        II. APU
        III. ICE
        IV. Seatbelts
        V. No smoking

    b. Engines
        I. Engine 1
        II. Engine 2

    c.Lights
        I. Cockpit lights
        II. Nav lights
        III. Landing lights
        IV. Taxi lights
        V. Cabin lights
        VI. Beacon lights
        VII. Strobe lights
        VIII. Logo lights

    d. Checklists
        I. Pre flight
            i. Parking brake(SET)
            ii.Battery(ON)
            iii.APU(ON)
            iv. No smoking(ON)
            v. Seatbelts(ON)
            vi. Fuel Quantity(KG)
            vii. Passenger door(CLOSED)
            viii. Cargo door(CLOSED)

        II. Engine start
            i. Beacon lights(ON)
            ii. Engine number 1(ON)
            iii. Engine number 2(ON)
            iv. APU(OFF)

        III. Before taxi
            i.Taxi lights(ON)
            ii. Nav lights(ON)

        IV. Before take-off
            i. Strobe lights(ON)
            ii. Take-off flaps(1 or 2)
            iii. Cabin lights(OFF)
            iv.Landing lights(ON)

        V. After take-off
            i. Positive rate(GEAR UP)
            ii. 210kts(FLAPS UP)

        VI. Climb-out
            i. Cabin ligts(ON)

        VII. Cruise and descent
            i. Cabin lights(OFF)
            ii. ICE(ON)
            iii. Landing lights(ON)
            
        VIII. Approach
            i. Spoilers(ARMED)
            ii.Landing Flaps(3 or FULL)
            
        IX. Landing
            i. Brakes(2)
            ii. Landing gear(DOWN)

        X. After landing
            i. Flaps(RETRACT)
            ii. Spoilers(DOWN)
            iii. Strobe lights(OFF)
            iv. ICE(OFF)
            v. Landing lights(OFF)
            vi.Taxi lights(ON)
            vii. APU(ON)

        XI. Parking
            i. Parking breaks(SET)
            ii. APU(OFF)
            iii. Engine number 1(OFF)
            iv. Engine number 2(OFF)
            v. Nav lights(OFF)
            vi. Beacon lights(OFF)
            vii. Battery(OFF)
            
5. Flaps (0, 1, 2, 3, FU)

6. Gear

7.Spoilers (Auto, 0, 1, 2, 3)

8. Parking (P, 1, 2, 3, M)
